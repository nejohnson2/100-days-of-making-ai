About

100 Days of Making AI is a creative challenge by Nicholas E. Johnson.

Each day for 100 days, I spend 1.5 hours building an AI tool or project. The goal isn't perfection — it's practice. Each project is self-contained and completed within the session. What matters is showing up and making something.

This site documents every project along the way. Click any tile on the home page to read about that day's creation.
