About

100 Days of Making AI is a creative challenge by Nicholas E. Johnson.

Each day for 100 days, I spend time building an AI tool or project. The goal isn't perfection — it's practice. Each project is self-contained and completed within the session. What matters is showing up and making something.

The projects vary widely in complexity and scope. Some are simple experiments, while others are more ambitious applications. The focus is on learning and exploring the capabilities of AI technologies.

This work is inspired by Katherine Dillon who teaches 100 Days of Making at NYU's ITP Program. Her initial work modifying Google Earth images to create stunning visual art was truly inspriational.

This site documents my projects along the way. Click any tile on the home page to read about that day's creation.
